Cookie Policy

A short, honest page about cookies on this site.

🍪
TL;DR

We use one piece of local storage (not even a cookie) to remember you've seen our notice. That's it.

What's actually used on this website

This is not the kind of site that loads twelve trackers and pretends a cookie banner makes that okay. We use exactly one entry in your browser's local storage:

timerdoro_cookie_acknowledged

Type: localStorage entry (not a cookie)
Purpose: Remember you've seen our notice so we don't show it again
Lifetime: Until you clear your browser data
Sent to anyone: No — never leaves your browser

What we don't do

  • No analytics cookies (no Google Analytics, Plausible, Fathom, etc.)
  • No advertising cookies
  • No tracking pixels
  • No social media embeds that drop cookies
  • No CDN-based fingerprinting
  • No A/B testing tools

We don't collect any analytics about who visits this site. We genuinely don't know how many people read this page. If you want to tell us you were here, just say hi.

How to remove the local storage entry

If you want to make sure even this single entry is gone:

  • Open browser settings → Privacy / Site Data → find raphael.wudernitz.at/raphael/timerdoro → Delete
  • Or: Open DevTools (right-click → Inspect) → Application → Local Storage → Delete the entry
  • Or: Use your browser's "Clear browsing data" feature

What about the Timerdoro app itself?

The app is a separate matter — and even there, we collect nothing. All your session data, settings, and preferences live in iOS's standard local storage on your own device. They never reach any server.

For full details, see our Privacy Policy.

Future changes

If we ever add anything that requires actual cookies (we don't plan to), we'll update this page and prominently mention it. For now: just one bookkeeping entry, and that's it.
