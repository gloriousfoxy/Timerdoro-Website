Why I built this

I tried every Pomodoro app on the App Store. Some were beautiful but lacked features I needed. Others had everything but felt cluttered or pushed me into expensive subscriptions for basic functionality. None felt right.

So I started building my own — initially just for myself. After friends tried early versions and asked when they could have it on their phones, I decided to make it real.

Timerdoro is the app I wished existed: clean, beautiful, native to Apple's ecosystem, with a fair price and zero compromises on privacy.

What I believe

🍅 Focus over features

An app should do one thing exceptionally well. Pomodoro is the core, everything else serves that.

🔒 Your data is yours

No analytics, no trackers, no third-party SDKs. What happens on your device stays on your device.

💎 Pay once, own it

Subscriptions for everything became absurd. Buy Timerdoro Pro once and keep it forever.

🎨 Design matters

You'll spend hours with this app. It deserves to be beautiful, not just functional.

🍎 Native is best

Built with SwiftUI for iPhone, iPad, and Mac. Each platform feels like home, not an afterthought.

♻️ Ship slowly, intentionally

I'd rather release one polished update than ten half-baked ones. Quality over quantity.

The roadmap

Timerdoro is at v1.0 now — the foundation. Here's what's next:

  • v1.1 — Apple Watch app (standalone usable, syncs with iPhone)
  • v1.1 — macOS Menu Bar with quick-access timer
  • v1.2 — Live Activities & Dynamic Island integration
  • v1.2 — Home Screen Widgets with interactive controls
  • v1.3 — iCloud sync across all your Apple devices
  • v2.0 — Ambient sounds & Apple Health integration
